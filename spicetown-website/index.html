Barbecue Buffet Restaurant, with a Live Mocktail bar and Exquisite Roof top Ambience in Namma Bengaluru. It's a beautiful Rooftop place which gives a beautiful view of the area in the mornings and night the ambience is lit with beautiful lights in the place.

We grill BBQ on the table in both veg and non-veg options. For the main course, there is a spread with Chinese, Indian and Italian cuisine. End the meal on a sweet note one can choose desserts available like pastries, Mousse and many more.
